# Account and Workspace Management

## Change your email
1. Open **Settings → Profile**
2. Update the email and confirm via the verification link

Workspace owners cannot change email to an address already used by another member.

## Transfer workspace ownership
Only the current owner can transfer ownership:
1. Invite the new owner if they are not already a member
2. Open **Settings → Team**
3. Choose **Transfer ownership** next to the member

## Delete a workspace
Workspace deletion is irreversible after a 14-day grace period.
1. Open **Settings → Danger zone → Delete workspace**
2. Type the workspace subdomain to confirm
3. Export data first if needed (**Settings → Data export**)

During the grace period, email support@cloudledger.example to cancel deletion.
