# Billing Plans and Pricing

CloudLedger offers three paid plans billed monthly or annually.

| Plan | Monthly | Annual (per month) | Seats included |
|------|---------|--------------------|----------------|
| Starter | $29 | $24 | 3 |
| Growth | $79 | $65 | 10 |
| Scale | $199 | $165 | 50 |

## What each plan includes
- **Starter**: expense tracking, invoices, basic reports
- **Growth**: approvals, multi-currency, API access (1,000 calls/day)
- **Scale**: SSO (SAML), custom roles, API access (20,000 calls/day), priority support

## Upgrading or downgrading
Go to **Settings → Billing → Change plan**. Upgrades take effect immediately and
are prorated. Downgrades take effect at the next renewal date.

## Trial
New workspaces receive a 14-day Growth trial. No credit card is required for the trial.
After the trial ends, the workspace moves to read-only until a plan is selected.
