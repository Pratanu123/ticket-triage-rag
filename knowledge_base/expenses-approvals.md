# Expenses and Approvals

## Submit an expense
1. Go to **Expenses → New**
2. Enter merchant, amount, category, and attach a receipt (PDF/PNG/JPG, max 10 MB)
3. Click **Submit for approval**

## Approval rules (Growth and Scale)
Admins can configure rules under **Settings → Approvals**:
- Amounts over a threshold require manager approval
- Specific categories can require finance approval
- Multi-step chains are supported on Scale

## Status meanings
- `draft` — not submitted
- `pending` — waiting on an approver
- `approved` — ready for reimbursement
- `rejected` — returned with a comment
- `reimbursed` — marked paid by finance

Approvers receive email and in-app notifications for pending items.
