# Frequently Asked Questions

## Can I change my workspace subdomain?
Yes, once every 30 days under **Settings → Workspace → Subdomain**. Old links
redirect for 90 days.

## Do you support multi-currency?
Growth and Scale support expenses and invoices in 30+ currencies. Conversion uses
daily mid-market rates from our FX provider. Starter is USD-only.

## Is there a desktop app?
No native desktop app. Use the web app; offline draft expenses are cached in the
browser for up to 7 days.

## How do I contact human support?
- Starter/Growth: support@cloudledger.example (business hours)
- Scale: priority queue via in-app chat plus email

Include your workspace subdomain and relevant invoice or expense IDs.

## What browsers are supported?
Latest Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge. Internet Explorer is not supported.
