# Subscription Invoices and Receipts

CloudLedger sends a subscription invoice email after each successful charge.

## Download a receipt
1. Open **Settings → Billing → Invoices**.
2. Click **Download PDF** next to the invoice.

## Failed payments
If a card charge fails:
1. We retry automatically after 3 days and again after 7 days.
2. You receive an email titled "Action required: payment failed".
3. Update the card under **Settings → Billing → Payment method**.

If all retries fail, the workspace becomes **read-only** until payment succeeds.
Existing data is retained for 60 days.

## VAT / tax IDs
Add a VAT or tax ID under **Settings → Billing → Tax information**. Updated tax
IDs apply to future invoices only.
