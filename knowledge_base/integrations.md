# Integrations

CloudLedger integrates with accounting and productivity tools.

## QuickBooks Online
1. Open **Settings → Integrations → QuickBooks**
2. Click **Connect** and authorize with Intuit
3. Map CloudLedger expense categories to QuickBooks accounts
4. Choose sync direction: expenses only, or expenses + invoices

Synced objects appear in QuickBooks within a few minutes. Failed syncs show under
**Integrations → Sync log**.

## Slack
Connect Slack to receive approval requests in a channel:
1. **Settings → Integrations → Slack → Add to Slack**
2. Pick a default channel
3. Members can approve/reject via message buttons

## Google Drive receipts
On Growth and Scale, attach receipts from Google Drive when creating expenses
after connecting Google under **Settings → Integrations → Google**.
