# Login and Password Reset

## Reset a forgotten password
1. Go to https://app.cloudledger.example/login
2. Click **Forgot password**
3. Enter your email and submit
4. Open the reset link within 30 minutes

If you do not see the email, check spam and confirm you are using the same address
as your CloudLedger account.

## Locked account
After 10 failed login attempts in 15 minutes, the account locks for 30 minutes.
You can still reset the password during a lockout.

## Session length
Browser sessions expire after 14 days of inactivity. SSO sessions follow your
identity provider's session policy.
