# Refund Policy

## Subscription refunds
- Monthly plans: refunds are available within 7 days of the charge if the
  workspace has sent fewer than 5 invoices during that period.
- Annual plans: refunds are available within 14 days of the initial annual charge.
  After 14 days, unused months are not refundable but you may cancel to stop renewal.

## How to request a refund
1. Open **Settings → Billing → Request refund**, or
2. Email billing@cloudledger.example with your workspace subdomain and invoice ID

Refunds are processed to the original payment method within 5–10 business days.

## Partial refunds
We do not offer partial-month refunds for voluntary downgrades mid-cycle. Seat
removals take effect at the next renewal.

## Chargebacks
If you file a chargeback, the workspace may be suspended while the dispute is
investigated. Contact support before disputing a charge so we can resolve it faster.
