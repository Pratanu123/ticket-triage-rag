# Troubleshooting Sync and Data Issues

## Invoices not sending
- Confirm the customer has a valid email
- Check **Settings → Email delivery** for bounce notices
- Verify your plan is not in read-only mode due to a failed payment

## Expenses stuck in pending
- Confirm an approver is assigned for the submitter's department
- Check that the approver still has an active seat
- Admins can reassign under **Expenses → Pending → Reassign**

## Duplicate customers
Merging is available under **Customers → Select duplicates → Merge**. The primary
record keeps its invoices; secondary records are archived.

## Export takes too long
Large workspaces (50k+ expenses) may take up to 1 hour for CSV export. You will
receive an email with a download link valid for 48 hours.
