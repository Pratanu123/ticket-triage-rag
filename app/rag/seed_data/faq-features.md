# FAQ: Product Features

## Boards and views
Each project supports Kanban, table, and calendar views. Custom fields
(dropdown, number, date, people) are available on Pro and Business.

## Automations
Pro+: rules like “when status becomes Done, notify Slack”. Business adds
multi-step automations and scheduled triggers.

## Integrations
Slack, GitHub, Jira import, Google Calendar, and Zapier. Connect under
**Settings → Integrations**.

## Reporting
Workload and burndown charts are on Pro+. CSV export of tasks is on all plans
under **Project → Export**.
