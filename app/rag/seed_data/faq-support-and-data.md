# FAQ: Support, Data, and Security

## Contact support
- Starter/Pro: support@cloudnova.example (business hours)
- Business: in-app chat + priority email

Include your workspace slug and relevant project or invoice IDs.

## Export or delete data
Owners can export all workspace data from **Settings → Data export** (link
emailed within 1 hour, valid 48 hours). Workspace deletion has a **14-day**
grace period under **Settings → Danger zone**.

## Security basics
- TLS 1.2+ in transit; AES-256 at rest
- MFA/2FA available on all plans
- SAML SSO on Business
- Report vulnerabilities to security@cloudnova.example
