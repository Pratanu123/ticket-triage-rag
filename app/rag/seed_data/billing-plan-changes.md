# Billing: Plan Upgrades and Downgrades

CloudNova plans: **Starter ($19/mo)**, **Pro ($49/mo)**, **Business ($129/mo)**.

## Upgrade
1. Open **Settings → Billing → Change plan**
2. Select Pro or Business
3. Confirm — upgrade is **immediate** and **prorated** for the remaining cycle

Pro unlocks the public API (5,000 calls/day). Business unlocks SSO and 50,000
API calls/day.

## Downgrade
Downgrades take effect at the **next renewal date**. You keep current-plan
features until then. If your usage exceeds the target plan (e.g. more seats than
Starter allows), remove extras before the renewal or the downgrade is blocked.

## Annual billing
Switch monthly → annual under **Billing → Cadence**. You receive a credit for
unused monthly time applied to the annual invoice.
