# Billing: Refunds

## Eligibility
- **Monthly plans**: refund within **7 days** of the charge if the workspace
  created fewer than 10 projects in that period.
- **Annual plans**: refund within **14 days** of the initial annual charge.
  After 14 days, unused months are not refundable; cancel to stop renewal.

## How to request a refund
1. Go to **Settings → Billing → Request refund**, or
2. Email billing@cloudnova.example with your workspace slug and invoice ID
   (format `inv_XXXX`).

Refunds return to the original payment method in **5–10 business days**.

## What is not refundable
- Seat removals mid-cycle (takes effect next renewal)
- Add-ons already consumed (extra AI credits, one-time migration packages)
- Chargebacks filed without contacting support first may suspend the workspace
  during investigation
